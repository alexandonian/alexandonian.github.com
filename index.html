About

Short introduction with some external links.


I'm currently a student at Bates College studying neuroscience. My advisors are Jason Castro and Travis Gould.

I will pursue a joint neuro-physics thesis investigating the molecular organization of the mouse olfactory system. In particular, I will analyze in-situ hybridization data provided by the Allen Brain Institute to quantify spatial patterning and clustering of gene expression across the OB's mitral cell layer, for a large fraction of the genome.

I also hope to TA undergraduate seminars in computational neuroscience and image processing in the upcoming academic year. Some of my interests are:

  • Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision
  • Deep Learning
  • Artificial Intelligence
